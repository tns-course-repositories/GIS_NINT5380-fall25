Week 14 Class 13 Overview

As we have discussed, this week will be an optional in-person final project work session. This will occur at the ‘D’ Building which is on 16th Street, in the middle of the block (south side) just west of Union Square. The 7th floor features several PC Lab rooms that have QGIS installed. We will plan to meet at the room #706, using #701 as a potential backup lab.

The work lab will start at 8pm Monday 12/01/25 and run until 9:50pm. This is a chance to get in-person guidance and feedback for the final project. This in not a mandatory meeting as it is in person, and our class is an on-line class. All students will be marked present for this class meeting regardless if they attend in-person or not.

Do note that no Zoom credentials will be made available; this is strictly an in-person session opportunity.
